Giving

Everything we do is made possible by people who choose to give generously.

Why it matters

Generosity at the heart of who we are

One of our core values is being a real and generous community — and that begins with how we steward the resources God has given us. Giving is an act of trust and worship, not just a financial transaction.

Everything we do at Coopers Edge Community Church — our Sunday gatherings, Coopers Kids, The Ark Toddler Group, community events, and all that we offer the neighbourhood — is made possible by people choosing to give regularly and generously.

Whether you give a little or a lot, your generosity directly enables us to grow as a community and serve Coopers Edge more effectively. Thank you.

Gift Aid

Boost your gift by 25%

If you're a UK taxpayer, you can increase the value of your donation by 25% at no extra cost to you through the Gift Aid scheme. For every £1 you give, we receive £1.25.

To Gift Aid your donation, please let us know by emailing info@cecommunitychurch.com. You'll need to confirm that you pay at least as much UK income tax or capital gains tax as the amount we'll claim on your donations.

Questions about giving?

We'd love to hear from you. Whether you have a question about Gift Aid, bank transfers, or anything else, just drop us a line.
