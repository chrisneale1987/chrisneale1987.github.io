29 March – 5 April 2026

Easter 2026

A week of events for the whole Coopers Edge community — everyone welcome.

Come and celebrate

Something for everyone

From a Palm Sunday parade to an Easter Egg Hunt, a Good Friday interactive experience to a celebration service with baptisms — there's something for every age across Holy Week. All events are free unless noted, and everyone is warmly welcome.

29
Mar

Palm Sunday

4:00 – 5:00 pm · Community Centre

Bring your scooter or bike to decorate as a donkey, make a palm leaf, hear the Palm Sunday story, and join the parade. A fun, memorable way to start Holy Week together as a community.

Free — all welcome
1
Apr

Easter Egg Hunt

10:00 am – 1:00 pm · Community Centre & around Coopers Edge

Take part in an egg hunt around Coopers Edge, starting from the Community Centre. Chocolate prizes to be won! £1 per entry — cash only please.

£1 entry · Cash only
3
Apr

Good Friday

10:00 – 11:30 am · School

Travel through five interactive stations from the Easter story, complete fun challenges, collect clues, and crack the secret code. A creative, hands-on experience suitable for all the family.

Free — all welcome
5
Apr

Easter Sunday

10:30 am – 12:00 pm · Community Centre

Start the day with a cooked breakfast roll, then join a short, joyful service including baptisms. He is risen — come and celebrate with us!

Free — all welcome
