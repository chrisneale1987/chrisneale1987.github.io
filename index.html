Welcome

We'd love to
meet you

Coopers Edge Community Church is a new, growing congregation officially launched in April 2024. We're a diverse, warm community centred on Jesus — gathering weekly to worship together, share life, and bring God's love to our neighbourhood.

Whether you're a long-time churchgoer, completely new to faith, or somewhere in between, there's a place for you here. Come as you are.

How we gather

1st Sunday The Gathering — all-age, exploring the big story of the Bible in creative and interactive ways, ending with a shared sandwich dinner
2nd & 4th Sunday EQUIP — relaxed gatherings designed to help us grow in faith, with worship, Bible discussion in age-tailored groups, and a simple sandwich dinner
3rd Sunday CELEBRATE — whole church family together with stories, encouragements, birthday celebrations, worship, Bible teaching, and communion

Who we are

Our values

01

Real & Generous Community

We aim to be honest and open with one another, reflecting the overwhelming generosity of God in all we do.

02

Founded in the Bible

We take God's word seriously, making biblical teaching accessible to all as we grow together in understanding Scripture.

03

Fuelled by the Spirit

We seek to be a community empowered by the Holy Spirit, transforming us to become more like Jesus and enabling mission.

04

On Mission for Coopers Edge

We exist to share God's love in this community — blessing our neighbours and the whole of Coopers Edge.
